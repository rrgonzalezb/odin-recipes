Chicken Gumbo

Ingredients

  • Celery
  • Green pepper
  • White onion
  • Garlic
  • Chicken thighs (chicken breasts are good too)
  • Vegetable oil
  • All purpose flour
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Dark Beer
  • Bay leaves
  • Parsley
  • Smoked sausage (optional but recommended!)
  • Accompany with some white rice!

Method

  1. Mise en place
